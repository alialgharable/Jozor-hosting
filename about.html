Our Roots

Weaving Heritage Into Modern Fashion

Founded in 2018, Falasteen Threads began as a passion project to preserve Palestinian embroidery (Tatreez) while providing sustainable livelihoods for refugee artisans. Our founder, Layla Al-Masri, grew up watching her grandmother weave stories through fabric.

Today, we bridge traditional Palestinian craftsmanship with contemporary design, creating pieces that honor our heritage while fitting modern lifestyles. Each garment tells a story - of resilience, identity, and hope.

Our Journey Through Time

Stitching memories, creating legacy

The Beginning

Founded in a small workshop with 5 artisans. First collection featured traditional Gaza embroidery.

Featured in international fashion blogs. Launched our e-commerce platform.

Community Growth

Expanded to 50 artisans. Started youth embroidery training programs.

Sustainability Focus

Transitioned to 100% organic materials. Implemented zero-waste production.
